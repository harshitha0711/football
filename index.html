⚽ Football Game

Score as many goals as you can!

Movement

W - Up | S - Down | A - Left | D - Right

Shooting

Press R to SHOOT

Hold SPACE to charge power before pressing R

How to Play:

  • Use W, A, S, D keys to move your player (blue)
  • The ball follows your player automatically
  • Press R to shoot the ball toward the goal (yellow box)
  • Hold SPACE to charge more power, then press R to shoot
  • Press W or S while shooting to curve the ball up or down
  • Avoid offside - don't go past the defenders too early!
  • Red players are defenders, Orange is the goalkeeper
  • Score points by getting the ball in the goal!
